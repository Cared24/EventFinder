About Event finder

Our Goal

Our main goal is to help you find new experiences, get to know previously unkown places, make new friends and have a wonderful time. And the best thing: EventFinder is completely free!

Planning

With EventFinder you can easily set up your preferencies for going out. You can see current day events or in the near future. Notifications can be sent to your liking.

Available

We currently can recommend events in the following cities in Hungary: Budapest, Szeged, Pécs and soon in Gyula.
Following your requests, we can soon be downloaded in other countries too!
